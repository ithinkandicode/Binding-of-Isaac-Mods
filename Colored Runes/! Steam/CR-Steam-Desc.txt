Only affects [b]runes on the ground[/b], not held runes. Changing the held icon would mean replacing the vanilla runes with custom versions (eg. like [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2848704602]this mod[/url] does, with "Morph"), but I'm hesitant to do that because I don't know exactly what the repurcusions might be, particularly how it might affect compatibility with other mods. So the approach I've taken here should be highly compatible and future-proof.

Obviously this is a bit of a cheat in shops but I mainly made this as a QoL thing.

Inspired by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2658081136]Visible Cards on the Floor[/url], and borrows some code from that mod.
